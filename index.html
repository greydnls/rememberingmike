Story

Mike Dugan was a vibrant person, a passionate developer and an active member of the PHP community. He blogged frequently, shared ideas and thoughts, had a sharp wit and a joke for every situation. He was also an amazing friend.

He passed away in his sleep, Sunday March 22, 2015. We lost one of the best of us that day, and he will be greatly missed. We're raising money for his family, to help with funeral expenses as well as anything else they may need.

If you would like to donate, please use Paypal to send money to rememberingmikedugan@gmail.com. If you don't want to use Paypal, but still would like to donate, please contact kayladnls@gmail.com.

Memories of Mike:

"Mike never ever let me get away with being less than I could be. He'd push me, and challenge me, and point out when I made a mistake. He'd also help me fix it, and make it better." -- Kayla Daniels

Questions

Where is the money going?

All of it will go directly to Mike's Family. They have direct access to the paypal account to use the funds as they see fit.

Why don't you use [insert funding platform name]?

kickstarter.com doesn't apply to fundraisers. All other options take a percentage off the top, and we'd rather see the money go directly to Mike's family. Paypal used to support personal fundraising but that seems to have vanished.
